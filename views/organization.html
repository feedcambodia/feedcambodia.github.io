About Feed Cambodia

Feed Cambodia is a brand new start-up charity organization founded by Chanthu Oeur. Our goal is to provide assistance to poverty-stricken people in Cambodia that are lacking life's essentials such as food, medicine, clothing, and shelter. We maximize the money going to those in need by keeping our costs low and spending your donations wisely.

Where Does the Money Go

We collect donations on our website using Paypal. They charge us 2.9% plus $.30 USD per transaction. Once we receive a group of donations, we wire money to Cambodia via Money Gram which can cost $10 or more. Our staff members in Cambodia collect the money and purchases the necessary goods that will go to individuals we have determined to be the most in need. In addition to Paypal and Money Gram fees, we will also incur transportation costs as part of our operations in Cambodia. Once needy individuals in Cambodia receive goods that will help them, documentation, photos, and/or video will be displayed on our website to show how the money was spent. We believe in full transparency as to how we use donation money. Please visit our Budget page for a full accounting of our expenditures.

Aware, Care, Share

Our goal of providing assistance to Cambodians in need is achieved by following three steps. Aware, Care, and Share:

Aware: Through the use of low-cost social media marketing and other web-based technology, we hope to foster awareness about the plight of many Cambodians that are in desperate need of assistance.

Care: Once we have reached out to individuals and they are aware of the situation in Cambodia, we hope they will care and want to do something about it.

Share: Those that care about what is going on in Cambodia may want to share. They can share by making a donation or by helping us spread the word through social media channels.

We Spend Wisely

At Feed Cambodia we like being thrift and not wasting money. We want to spend more donation money on people in Cambodia that need help, and less on running our organization. These are the ways we save money.

Office: We don't have an office. We run our organization virtually out of our homes, from the local coffee shop, or wherever. We just need a computer and Internet access, and we are good-to-go. That way we don't waste money on rent, office equipment, electric bills, etc.

Staff: We don't have a board of directors, high-paid executives, or administrative staff. It's just me, Chanthu Oeur running things here in the U.S. and my brother Yoeut and his son Chetra managing things in Cambodia.

Technology: Our technology parter, Lahuna, is developing our website using free open-source software that doesn't require licensing fees. Lahuna has donated hundreds of hours of programming time, saving us thousands of dollars.

Marketing: Currently we don't use any paid advertising. We rely on word-of-mouth and sharing through social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter, and Google.
