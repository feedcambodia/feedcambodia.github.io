Feed Cambodia is a financially-responsible, technology-based, charity organization that raises funds to assist poverty-stricken people in Cambodia. We strive to keep our costs low so more donation funds go to those that need it. We utilize word-of-mouth and the power of social media sharing to raise awareness about the difficult life facing many Cambodians today.

You can help by making a donation, endorsing us, or spreading the word about our cause through social media such as Facebook.

Donate: Donations, however small, go a long way and will make a huge difference.

Share: Help us spread the word by sharing our cause across the Web.

Endorse: If you believe in our cause and trust our organization, please endorse us.
