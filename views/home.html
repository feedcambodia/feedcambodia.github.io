Hello, my name is Chanthu Oeur and I created feedcambodia.com to raise awareness about the plight of poverty-stricken people in Cambodia. Cambodia's poor face many problems in their daily lives such as malnutrition, disease, and human trafficking. The scope of the problems there is so large, that many individuals remain unaided by humanitarian efforts and are in desperate need of assistance. You can help by sharing information about my cause through word-of-mouth and social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Thank you!
