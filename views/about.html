About Feed Cambodia

Feed Cambodia is a brand new start-up charity organization founded by Chanthu Oeur. Our goal is to provide assistance to poverty-stricken people in Cambodia that are lacking life's essentials such as food, medicine, clothing, and shelter. We maximize the money going to those in need by keeping our costs low and spending your donations wisely.


Aware, Care, Share


Our goal of providing assistance to Cambodians in need is achieved by three steps. Aware, Care, and Share:


Aware: Through the use of low-cost social media marketing and other web-based technology, we hope to foster awareness about the plight of many Cambodians that are in desperate need of assistance.

Care: Once we have reached out to individuals and they are aware of the situation in Cambodia, we hope they will care and went to do something about it.

Share: Those that care about what is going on in Cambodia may want to share. They can share by making a donation or by helping us spread the word through social media channels.
